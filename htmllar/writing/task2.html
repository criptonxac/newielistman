Writing Task 2

You should spend about 40 minutes on this task. Write at least 250 words.
Recommended time allocation: 20 minutes for Task 1, 40 minutes for Task 2

Essay Question:

Some people believe that the internet has brought people closer together by enabling them to communicate with others around the world. Others argue that the internet has actually made people more isolated and less connected to their local communities.

Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.

Key Requirements:

  • Write at least 250 words
  • Discuss both views presented in the question
  • Give your own opinion clearly
  • Support your points with reasons and examples
  • Use formal academic language
  • Organize your essay with clear paragraphs
  • Use linking words and cohesive devices

Essay Structure Suggestion:

  1. Introduction: Paraphrase the question and state your opinion
  2. Body Paragraph 1: Discuss the first view (internet brings people together)
  3. Body Paragraph 2: Discuss the second view (internet isolates people)
  4. Body Paragraph 3: Your opinion with strong examples
  5. Conclusion: Summarize main points and restate opinion

Task 2 Writing Tips:

  • Plan your essay before writing (2-3 minutes)
  • Write 4-5 paragraphs with clear topic sentences
  • Use examples from your experience or general knowledge
  • Include linking words: Furthermore, However, On the other hand, In conclusion
  • Check grammar and spelling in your final 2 minutes
  • Task 2 is worth twice as much as Task 1 - spend more time here

Useful Language for "Discuss Both Views" Essays:

Introducing views: "Proponents argue that...", "Critics contend that...", "It is often claimed that..."

Contrasting: "However", "On the other hand", "Conversely", "In contrast"

Your opinion: "In my view", "I believe that", "From my perspective"

Examples: "For instance", "A clear example is", "This can be seen in"
